# Beautiful Mathematics with LaTeX

This blog post demonstrates how to render beautiful mathematical equations using LaTeX syntax.

## Basic Inline Math

You can write inline math like $E = mc^2$ or $\pi \approx 3.14159$ directly in your text.

## Block Equations

For more complex equations, use block display:

$$\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}$$

## Complex Mathematical Expressions

### Quadratic Formula

The solutions to $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$ are:

$$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$$

### Matrix Operations

A $2 \times 2$ matrix multiplication:

$$\begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} ax + by \\ cx + dy \end{bmatrix}$$

### Summation and Series

The famous series:

$$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6}$$

### Calculus

The derivative of $f(x) = x^n$ is:

$$\frac{d}{dx}x^n = nx^{n-1}$$

And the integral:

$$\int x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C \quad (n \neq -1)$$

### Advanced: Fourier Transform

The Fourier transform of a function $f(t)$ is:

$$F(\omega) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(t) e^{-i\omega t} dt$$

### Statistics

The normal distribution probability density function:

$$f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}} e^{-\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{x-\mu}{\sigma}\right)^2}$$

## Why LaTeX?

LaTeX provides:

- **Professional typography** for mathematical expressions
- **Consistent formatting** across all equations
- **Complex structures** like fractions, matrices, integrals
- **Beautiful rendering** that matches academic standards

This makes mathematical content much more readable and professional-looking compared to plain text alternatives.
